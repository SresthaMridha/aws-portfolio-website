About

I design and validate production-grade AWS architectures focused on high availability, security, and cost optimization. My approach emphasizes resilience, scalability, and operational discipline under real-world failure conditions.

Architecture Philosophy

My infrastructure design follows a layered, high-availability model: Users → Application Load Balancer → Stateless EC2 (Auto Scaling) → Private RDS (Multi-AZ) → S3 for storage, logs, and backups.

Systems are designed to tolerate AZ failure, instance failure, and traffic spikes without manual intervention.
